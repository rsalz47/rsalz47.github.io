About

Hey there. I'm a reverse engineer by trade and a tech junkie by choice.

I've got two fancy papers in Computer Science from The University of Massachusetts, Amherst that say I'm certified to sit behind a screen, and I've developed a great deal of expertise in that regard. My professional interests lie in low-level security along the software-hardware line, with preference tipping ever so slightly towards the former. I have projects that focus on system design and implementation, such as a software simulation of a MIPS-1 CPU, a tree-walk interpreter written in Java, and a rudimentary kernel.

Beyond work, I also sit in front of screens recreationally. I'm a fan of unique narratives in interactive mediums, and play a lot of games that fit this bill. I often work through long game series in order and write about my opinions as they go on, which you can find under the Blog section of this website.
