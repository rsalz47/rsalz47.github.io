About

Hey there. I'm a reverse engineer by trade and a tech junkie by choice.

I've got two fancy papers in Computer Science from The University of Massachusetts, Amherst that say I'm certified to sit behind a screen, and I've developed a great deal of expertise in that regard. My professional interests lie in low-level security along the software-hardware line, with preference tipping ever so slightly towards the former. I have projects that focus on system design and implementation, such as a software simulation of a MIPS-1 CPU, a bytecode compiler written in Java, and a rudimentary kernel.
