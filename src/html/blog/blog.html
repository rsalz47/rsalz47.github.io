While I do plenty of writing for work, all of it is technical and therefore boring. I love making a well-crafted powerpoint as much as the next person, but that can only become so much of a creative exercise before your boss takes you off the project. To make up for that, I use this space to talk openly, freely and without financial risk. Expect plenty of talk about giant robots, video game narratives and blogging. Perhaps a pinch of creative prose, too.

Inspired by the recent conclusion of Xenoblade Chronicles, I dedicated 2023 to The Xenoyear, an excuse to explore the back catalog of the broader Xeno franchise. I wrote down my thoughts along the way, and have been trying to condense them into something others can reasonably interact with. I'm still working through some of those pieces, but feel free to follow along as I finish them up! The Xenoyear posts start here, with more to come.
