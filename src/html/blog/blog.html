I've found a lot to say about the everyday.

Over the past few years, I've been interested in paying closer attention to the stuff I read, watch, play and live. Authors, artists and developers put a lot of effort to focus and create the media they worked on; I'm more than willing to match some fraction of that and give some thought to what they're putting out. I also just enjoy writing down the day-to-day. This is the place where I attempt to organize all that into something presentable.

If you're looking for a place to jump into, you may want to check out the first post in The XenoyearTM. Coming off the heels of Xenoblade Chronicles 3's conclusory entrance in the back half of yesteryear, I've nominated this year of 2023 as an appropriate time to explore Tetsuya Takahashi's vast Xeno franchise, from its origins in Xenogears up until its premature ending in Xenosaga Episode 3: Also Sprach Zarathustra.
