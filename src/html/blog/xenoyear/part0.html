The XenoYear Part 0 - Familiar and Foreign

Most of the games I played growing up fell into the camp of big on text and turn based in gameplay. While I didn't grow up on the archetypal Final Fantasy or Dragon Quest directly, I certainly walked a parallel path in the Bravely Default, Fire Emblem and Persona franchises. As a result, I got well acquainted with the classic tropes of many Japanese RPG stories: often feudal settings, young adolescents, power of teamwork, evil cults, the final boss is god. This is a well-worn path and I've made my way down it many times.
